Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026 · Contact: haijayousif@gmail.com

TootieTry (“the app”, “we”) is a personal virtual try-on and wardrobe app. This policy explains what we collect, how it’s used, who it’s shared with, and how you can delete it.

What we collect

  • Account info — the email address and password you sign up with (the password is handled by our authentication provider; we never see it in plain text).
  • Photos you upload — a full-body photo of yourself and photos of your clothing.
  • Wardrobe data you create — item names, categories, colors, tags, prices, saved outfits, wear logs, and outfit plans.
  • Approximate location — only if you grant permission, used once to fetch local weather for outfit suggestions. It is not stored or linked to your account.

We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

How your data is used

Your data is used solely to provide the app’s features: storing your closet, rendering outfits onto your photo, suggesting looks, and tracking wear. We do not sell your data.

Who we share data with (service providers)

  • Supabase — hosts our database, file storage, and authentication. Your photos and wardrobe data are stored here under your account, isolated by row-level security.
  • FASHN — a third-party AI service. When you tap “Try on,” your body photo and the selected garment images are sent to FASHN, which returns the rendered try-on image. The app asks for your explicit consent before this happens the first time.
  • A background-removal service (remove.bg or Replicate) — garment images are sent to cut out their background and detect colors.
  • Open-Meteo — weather data by approximate coordinates; no personal identifiers sent.

These providers process your images only to perform the function above.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data until you delete it. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app: You → Delete account. This removes your profile, photos (from storage), closet items, saved outfits, try-on history, wear logs, and plans, and deletes your login. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Children

TootieTry is not directed at children and is not intended for use by children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by a new effective date.

Common topics

  • Set your body photo — open the You tab and add one clear, full-body photo.
  • Add clothes — in Closet, photograph each garment; the background is removed and colors detected automatically.
  • Try on an outfit — in Try-On, choose pieces and tap generate; each garment takes a few seconds to render.
  • Delete your account — You → Delete account permanently removes your account and all data.
