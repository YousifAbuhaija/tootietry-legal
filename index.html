Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

TootieTry (“the app,” “we,” “us”) is a personal virtual try-on app made for a small number of private users. We collect only what the app needs to work, we never sell your data, and we show no ads. This policy explains what we collect and who we share it with.

Information we collect

  • Account email — used to create your account and sign you in.
  • Photos you add — your full-body photo and photos of your own clothing.
  • Approximate location — only if you enable weather-based outfit suggestions; used to look up local weather. It is not stored with your account.
  • Wardrobe data you enter — item names, categories, colors, tags, prices, saved outfits, and wear history.

We do not collect advertising identifiers, your contacts, or browsing history, and we do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

How your information is used, and who processes it

To provide the app’s features, we use the following service providers:

  • Supabase — stores your account, photos, and wardrobe data. Your data is isolated to your own account by per-user access rules. (privacy)
  • FASHN — to render a try-on, your body photo and the garment images you select are sent to FASHN to generate the result image. (privacy)
  • Replicate — when you remove a garment’s background or detect its colors, that garment image is sent to Replicate. (privacy)
  • Open-Meteo — if weather suggestions are enabled, your approximate coordinates are sent to fetch the forecast. No account or identifier is shared. (terms)

Each provider processes your data only to deliver the requested feature, under its own privacy policy (linked above).

Storage & security

Photos are stored in Supabase Storage under your own account folder, and your data is protected by per-user access rules. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Notifications

The optional daily outfit reminder is a local notification scheduled on your device. We do not run a push server and do not collect push/notification tokens.

Keeping or deleting your data

You can delete individual garments, photos, and saved outfits in the app at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, email us at the address below and we’ll remove it.

Children

TootieTry is not directed to children and is not intended for use by children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date above reflects the current version.

Common topics

  • Set your body photo — open the You tab and add one clear, full-body photo.
  • Add clothes — in Closet, photograph each garment; the background is removed and colors detected automatically.
  • Try on an outfit — in Try-On, choose pieces and tap generate; each garment takes a few seconds to render.
  • Delete your account — email us and we’ll remove your account and data.
